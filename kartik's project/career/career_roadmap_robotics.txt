Career Roadmap for Embedded & Robotics Engineer (Next 1–3 Years)

--------------------------------------------------
0–1 Year (Current Stage)
--------------------------------------------------
Focus:
- Build strong base (C/C++ for embedded, ROS2 Nav2, URDF, RViz, sensor integration)
- Create 2–3 robotics side projects (mini SLAM robot, PCB motor driver, ESP32 + micro-ROS)
- Share projects on GitHub & LinkedIn

Salary:
- 3–5 LPA (current ~4.2 LPA)

Switch Opportunity:
- After 12 months → target 7–9 LPA in robotics/embedded companies

--------------------------------------------------
1–2 Years
--------------------------------------------------
Focus:
- Advanced SLAM & Localization (Cartographer, EKF/UKF, sensor fusion)
- Jetson/Raspberry Pi/NUC robotics projects
- Contribute to open-source ROS2 packages
- AI + Computer Vision (OpenCV, YOLO, TensorRT)
- Attend robotics hackathons / conferences

Salary:
- 8–12 LPA in product robotics companies (GreyOrange, Addverb, iRobot India, startups)

Switch Opportunity:
- Strong demand for combined hardware + software + ROS2 navigation engineers

--------------------------------------------------
2–3 Years
--------------------------------------------------
Focus:
- Specialize in one area:
  1. Robotics Navigation Specialist (SLAM, Path Planning)
  2. Embedded AI Engineer (ML on Jetson/STM32/ESP32)
  3. Robotics Hardware + Firmware Lead (PCB, drivers, ROS2 integration)
- Publish technical blogs/articles (LinkedIn, Medium)
- Mentor juniors or interns
- Consider certifications (NVIDIA Jetson AI, ROS2 Industrial, PCB design)

Salary:
- 12–18 LPA in India
- Niche R&D roles may reach 20–25 LPA
- International opportunities (Germany, Canada, Singapore)

--------------------------------------------------
Key Notes
--------------------------------------------------
- Don’t stay >2 years in a company if growth is slow
- Portfolio > Degree → GitHub, LinkedIn, hackathons matter
- Networking: ROS Discourse, Reddit r/robotics, meetups
- Consistency = top 10% robotics engineers in India within 3 years
