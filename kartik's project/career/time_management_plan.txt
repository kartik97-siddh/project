Weekly Time Management Plan for Upskilling & Projects

----------------------------------
Weekday Routine (Mon–Fri)
----------------------------------
7:00 – 7:30 PM   → Freshen up + light dinner/snack
7:30 – 9:00 PM   → Deep Focus Block (Upskilling/Projects)
   - Mon/Wed/Fri: Coding / ROS2 / Algorithms
   - Tue/Thu: Hardware / Electronics / PCB / Embedded projects
9:00 – 9:30 PM   → Dinner / Relax / Socialize with PG friends
9:30 – 10:30 PM  → Light Learning / Reading
   (Tutorials, docs, notes, robotics concepts)
10:30 – 11:00 PM → Chill (music, walk, family call)
11:00 PM – 7:00 AM → Sleep (essential for focus)

----------------------------------
Weekend Routine
----------------------------------
Saturday (Project Day)
- 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM → Side project building (robotics/PCB)
- Afternoon → Rest + social activities
- 7:00 PM – 10:00 PM → Debug, document progress on GitHub/LinkedIn

Sunday (Recharge + Light Learning)
- Morning → Sports/exercise/walk
- Afternoon (2–3 hrs) → Learning (lectures, courses)
- Evening → Relax, plan next week

----------------------------------
Tips to Stay Consistent
----------------------------------
- Use Pomodoro (25–30 min focus, 5 min break)
- Track progress in Notion/Obsidian journal
- Break projects into small goals
- Avoid social media after office hours

----------------------------------
Goal: ~10–12 hrs of focused learning per week without burnout.
